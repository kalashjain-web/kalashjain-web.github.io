Research Interests

My research examines how informational and institutional frictions – such as gaps in financial statements, misaligned incentives, and reliance on rigid classification systems – shape information processing and capital market responses. I study these issues in the context of financial reporting, asset management, and executive pay design.

Research Areas: Financial Statement Analysis & Valuation, Asset Management, CEO Compensation
